The Definitive Desert Island Movie Companions

What makes a memorable movie experience? The ending house scene in "The Mechanic"? Clarice Starling talking to Hannibal in the jail? The porch chimes in "Body Heat"? The last scene of Harry Caul in "The Conversation" or of Matty Walker in "Body Heat"? The piano scene in "The Fabulous Baker Boys"? Catherine Tramell's ice pick in "Basic Instinct"? That famous line in "Natural Born Killers" - "They know the difference between right and wrong; they just don't care"? Or the final 30 seconds of "The Sixth Sense"? What do you remember? Why does a particular movie experience bring a smile to your face? It's a very personal choice.
